# Rubric — brand-voice

Voice/tone/format compliance for [ARTIST] + Rising Tides captions and carousel copy. Derived from the `brand-voice` skill + `Memory/Content Patterns/Viral Formulas.md` + the "never let it feel like promotion" rule.

**Applies to:** `caption`, `carousel`, `script`, `hook`. NOT budget/creator-list/batch (those have their own rubrics).

---

## Rules

### MUST — hook lands in the first 3 seconds / first line
`rule_id: hook-in-first-line`
The first line of the caption (or first 3 seconds of a script) must be the hook — a scroll-stopper, not a throat-clearing greeting, label intro, or context-setter. If the first line reads "Hey guys", "So today", "Did you know", "Just wanted to", or any label/artist name lead-in, that's a hook buried. Viral Formulas Formula structure: Hook → Body → Close. The hook is line 1.

### MUST NOT — promotional framing
`rule_id: no-promo-language`
[ARTIST]'s voice is **oblique, never promotional**. FAIL if any of these appear: "stream our new", "check out", "drop Friday", "out now", "link in bio" as the lead, " smash that", "hit the", "don't forget to", "make sure you", "GO STREAM" / all-caps CTAs, or describing the song as a "banger"/"hit"/"fire". The CTA, when present, must be woven in (e.g. "pre-save [TRACK B]" mid-caption), not announced. Brand voice skill rule: *never make it feel like promotion.*

### MUST — [ARTIST] CTA present (captions only)
`rule_id: mon-cta-required`
Every caption must contain a soft, on-brand CTA tied to the [ARTIST] catalog or a pre-save/save/listen action. Acceptable: "pre-save [track]", "save [track]", "listen to [track]", "[TRACK B]", "[TRACK A]", "[TRACK C]". Bare "link in bio" with no song reference = FAIL. N/A for carousel copy unless the carousel is standalone (no caption attached).

### MUST NOT — emoji clutter
`rule_id: no-emoji-clutter`
Voice allows **≤1 emoji per caption**, and only if it earns its place (a 🌊 at a water/baptism reference, a 🛻 for a truck-scenic). FAIL if: ≥3 emojis in one caption, any emoji string (two+ in a row), or emojis that aren't tied to content (🔥, ✨, 🙌 as filler). Brand voice = restrained, regional, visual; emoji spam reads as a different account.

### MUST — consistent POV / no brand-schizophrenia
`rule_id: single-pov`
The caption must hold one point of view and tone register — either the artist's first-person, the narrator's second-person ("you"), or RT's operator-voice third-person. Mixing "I wrote this song" + "you'll love this track" + "RT broke another record" in one caption = three voices, FAIL. Pick one.

### MUST NOT — off-brand subject matter
`rule_id: on-brand-subject`
[ARTIST] subjects: the truck/Appalachia/baptism/[CULTURAL THEME]/loss-rebirth catalog, faith/doubt, the road, blue-collar labor, specific songs. FAIL if the caption drifts to: generic hustle/wealth/manifestation tropes, crypto, politics (unless tied to a specific song's content), other artists' lyrics, or trend-jacking unrelated to the catalog.

### SHOULD — line break / readability (informational)
`rule_id: readable-spacing`
Informational only (does not FAIL on its own). Captions over 4 lines with no line breaks, or single blocks over 280 chars, hurt completion. Flag as a SHOULD, not a MUST.
